# TASK-3 : Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding

## Objective

This task focuses on performing an in-depth business analysis by defining Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), exploring business trends, and developing an interactive dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset contains Superstore sales transaction details including:

* Customer Information
* Product Information
* Sales Details
* Shipping Details
* Profit & Discount Information

Total Records: **9,994 Rows**
Total Columns: **21 Columns**

---

# Operations Performed

## KPI Definition

Defined key business metrics to evaluate overall business performance:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Average Discount

---

## Deep-Dive Business Analysis

Performed detailed analysis on different business aspects:

* Product Performance Analysis
* Category-wise Sales & Profit Analysis
* Region-wise Sales Performance
* Customer Sales Analysis
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

## Interactive Dashboard Development

Developed an interactive dashboard using Plotly to visualize business performance.

### Dashboard KPIs

* Total Sales

### Interactive Visualizations

* Sales by Region

## Business Insights

Generated meaningful insights from the dashboard:
